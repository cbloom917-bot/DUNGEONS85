PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 2026

Dungeons '85 is a minimalist, system-agnostic Virtual Tabletop (VTT). We value your privacy and believe in a "no-nonsense" approach to your data.

What Data We Access

When you use our "Load from Drive" feature, Dungeons '85 requests permission to access specific files stored in your Google Drive. We only access the files you explicitly select for your game session.

Data Storage & Usage

We do not store your data. Dungeons '85 does not maintain a database of user accounts, emails, or personal information. Your game assets, maps, and tokens remain entirely within your Google Drive. No game data, private files, or personal information is ever transmitted to or stored on our servers.

If you have any questions, please contact us via the CONTACT page.
