FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT IS DUNGEONS85?

Dungeons '85 is a minimalist, system-agnostic Virtual Tabletop (VTT). It is designed to get out of your way and let you focus on the game, using your own assets directly from Google Drive.

DO I NEED TO UPLOAD MY MAPS TO YOUR SERVER?

No. We do not host your images. You load your maps and tokens directly from your own Google Drive and "share" the links to those files with your players. Once you close the session, the connection is severed.

WHICH TTRPG SYSTEMS DOES IT SUPPORT?

Dungeons '85 is system-agnostic. If you have the maps, tokens, and dice, you can play anything. It is optimized for old school playstyles that prioritize playability over automation.

WHY CAN'T I SEE MY GOOGLE DRIVE FOLDERS?

The Google Picker API displays your files as Google organizes them. For the best experience, we recommend keeping your game assets in a dedicated "Shared Folder" so you can find them quickly during play.

HOW DOES THE TORCH TIMER WORK?

The Torch Timer is a tool for Shadowdark GMs to track one hour of real time (minus 0-300 seconds). It is a local-only timer, player do not see it. When used in alongside the Fog of War feature, it "Fogs" the entire map when the torch goes out.

CAN I PLAY ON MOBILE?

Dungeons '85 is designed for desktop play. While it will load on mobile, it is not optimized for small touchscreens.

WHERE IS MY DATA SAVED?

Nowhere. We do not store your game state, your player list, or your character sheets. Everything exists in the moment of your session.
